About Generations
  • filter_dramaWhat is Generations?

    What is Generations?

    Generations is a site created for the Personal Project, that allows senior citizens to post yardwork jobs and have UCC boys complete them in exchange for volunteer hours. This site is intented to help the elederly, better the community, and connect two distinct generations, hence the name "Generations."

  • placeWhy Generations?

    Why Generations?

    Generations is the perfect site for all seniors to use to post jobs, get help with yardwork, and make connections. With over 600 users, there is always a job and connection for you!

  • whatshotHow do I use Generations?

    How do I use Generations?

    If you are a senior citizen in need of yardwork, post a job on our Post A Job page. If you are a UCC boy, select a job and complete it in exchange for hours.
